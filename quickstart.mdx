### What is the Merchant Portal?

The Merchant Portal is the control center for managing your EV charging stations on the PlugQuest platform.

It allows you to list your charging stations, manage pricing, monitor usage in real time, and track your earnings—all from one simple dashboard.

Whether you operate a single charger or a full charging network, the Merchant Portal gives you full visibility and control over your infrastructure.

### Who is this for?

The Merchant Portal is designed for:

- EV charging station owners
- Businesses with installed charging infrastructure (malls, offices, hotels, apartments)
- Energy and utility partners
- Fleet operators managing multiple charging points
- Individuals who want to monetize their home or private chargers

If you provide access to EV charging, this platform is built for you.

### Key Features

With the Merchant Portal, you can:

### 📍 Station Management

- Add and manage multiple charging stations
- Update station details, location, and availability
- Upload images and descriptions for visibility

### 🔌 Charger Control

- Add multiple chargers per station
- Set charger type and power capacity
- Enable or disable chargers in real time

### 💰 Pricing & Revenue

- Set your own charging prices
- View earnings per session and per charger
- Track total revenue over time

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard

- Monitor charging activity in real time
- View usage trends and peak hours
- Understand performance across all stations

### ⚙️ Session Tracking

- See active and completed charging sessions
- View session duration and energy usage
- Handle session-related issues easily

### Requirements

To use the Merchant Portal, you need the following:

- A registered account on the PlugQuest platform
- A functional EV charging station (hardware installed and powered)
- Accurate location details for each station
- Pricing information for your charging services
- A valid payout method (bank account or supported payment option)

Optional but recommended:

- High-quality images of your charging station
- Stable internet connection for real-time monitoring
- Verified business or ownership details (for trust and visibility)
