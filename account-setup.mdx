The PlugQuest Merchant Portal provides entities in the EV businesses with a streamlined platform to manage their products, transactions, and customer interactions. To begin using the platform, merchants must first create an account via the registration interface.

## \
Account Registration

To create an account on the PlugQuest Merchant Portal:

1. Navigate to the registration page from the homepage. [https://merchant.plugquest.app/account/register](https://merchant.plugquest.app/account/register)
2. Provide the following required information:
   - **First Name** – Your given name
   - **Last Name** – Your surname
   - **Email Address** – A valid email address used for account verification, notifications, and login
   - **Country Code** – Default is set to **GH (\+233)** and can be changed if needed
   - **Phone Number** – An active mobile number used for OTP verification and account recovery
   - **Password** – A secure password for your account

### Password Requirements

Your password should include:

- A minimum of 8 characters
- Uppercase and lowercase letters
- At least one number
- At least one special character

### Submit Registration

After completing all required fields:

1. Click **Create Account**.
2. The system will validate the information provided.
3. If successful, your account will be created and verification steps may be required, including:
   - Email verification

### Existing Users

If you already have an account, click **Login** at the bottom of the page to access your account.

## Creating an Entity

After completing account registration, users are redirected to the **Entity Setup** page to create their organization profile within PlugQuest.

### What is an Entity?

An Entity represents a business, company, or EV charger owner within the PlugQuest platform. It serves as the primary organizational account responsible for managing charging operations and related resources.

### Purpose of an Entity

The Entity acts as the central management unit for:

- Managing multiple charging stations and locations
- Monitoring operational performance
- Managing user roles, access, and permissions
- Configuring business-level settings and preferences

### Entity Capabilities

Creating an Entity allows your organization to:

- Own and manage multiple charging stations
- Operate as the primary account holder within the platform
- Maintain business-related information, including company details, contact information, and billing records
- Manage charging infrastructure from a centralized dashboard
