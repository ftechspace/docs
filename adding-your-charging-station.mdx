---
title: "Adding Your Charging Station"
---

A **station** is a physical location where EV drivers can charge their vehicles.

This guide walks you through adding a station and configuring at least one charger so your station can go live.

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:

- Access to the Merchant Portal
- The station’s physical address
- Location coordinates (or be at the location)
- Access to the charger
- Internet connection (WiFi or Ethernet)
- Optional: images of the station

## Steps to Add a Station

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 04 17 124426](/images/Screenshot-2026-04-17-124426.jpg)
</Frame>

### 1. On the sidebar click **Stations**

### **2. Click the \+ Add Station button (top right)**

### **3.** Enter Basic Information

**Station Name**\
Give your station a clear name\
Example: _“City Mall Charging Hub”_

**Location Type**

- Public → Available to all drivers
- Private → Restricted

**Business Address**\
Enter the full address of the station

💡 _Tip:_ A clear address helps drivers find your station easily

**Set Location (Coordinates)**

You have 2 options:

**Option 1: Use Current Location**

- Click _Use Current Location_ if you're at the station

**Option 2: Enter Manually**

- Add Latitude and Longitude manually

📌 _Important:_ Accurate location ensures your station appears correctly on the map

**Add Images (Optional)**

- Upload photos of your station
- You can add multiple images

💡 _Tip:_ Good images increase trust and usage

### 4. Configure Charger 

After saving your station, you’ll need to add and configure at least one charger.

👉 Choose:

- **Save & Add Charger** (recommended)
- Or add a charger later from the station page

  ![Screenshot 2026 04 17 124510](/images/Screenshot-2026-04-17-124510.jpg)
</Frame>

### 5. Add Charger Details

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 04 17 124757](/images/Screenshot-2026-04-17-124757.jpg)
</Frame>

**Charger Name**\
Example: _“Charger 1”_ or _“Bay A”_

**Charger Type**

- DC → Fast charging
- AC → Standard charging

Click **Add Charger** to continue

### 6. Connect Charger (Physical Setup)

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 04 17 124845](/images/Screenshot-2026-04-17-124845.jpg)
</Frame>

Make sure:

- Charger is powered ON
- Connected to the internet
  - Ethernet (recommended)
  - Or WiFi

### Configure Charger Connection

1. Open your charger’s settings (via its IP address)
2. Go to **OCPP / Server Settings**
3. Paste the **WebSocket URL** provided in the portal
4. Save settings

📌 This connects your charger to PlugQuest

### Verify Connection

1. Restart the charger
2. Wait 2–5 minutes
3. Check status in the portal

👉 When status shows **Connected**, setup is complete
